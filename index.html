Frequently Asked Questions

Cyberbirds is an educational club designed to teach penetration testing skills while emphasizing ethical guidelines. You'll learn the basics of hacking in a legal and responsible manner.

No prior experience is required! We welcome anyone with an interest in cybersecurity. Our curriculum is designed to accommodate all skill levels.

We cover network fundamentals, computer engineering, cybersecurity, and cryptology. Programming languages include Bash, Python, HTML, and PHP.
