CYBERBIRDS

- At Johns Hopkins University -

Cyberbirds is a student-led club starting in the Spring of 2025 that aims to provide and study the tools used in the field of Cybersecurity. We will implement Hands-on activities that will cover topics such as networks, firewalls, penetration testing, cryptology, and more. We will also be hosting Capture the Flag competitions and eventually create an internship funnel for our members.

Thank you for checking us out!
